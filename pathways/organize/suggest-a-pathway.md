# Suggest a Pathway

Help grow the pathways index by suggesting a new contribution pathway. You can point to an existing process already documented in a team handbook, or share a contribution idea that's supported by a team.

- **Reference:** [About Pathways](https://make.wordpress.org/handbook/pathways/about/) — what makes a good pathway
- **Connect:** Join [#core-program](https://wordpress.slack.com/archives/core-program) and introduce yourself

## Steps

1. **Check if it already exists.** Search the [Pathways project board](https://github.com/orgs/WordPress/projects/282) to make sure your idea isn't already there. If it is, add feedback or volunteer to draft a guide.

2. **Confirm it's based on existing work.** Ask yourself: is this idea based on an existing, already-maintained process or team? If it's something new that would need ongoing maintenance, it'll need team vetting first.

3. **Gather relevant context.** Collect any relevant links — handbook pages, team channels, documentation, or examples that support your idea.

4. **File a New Pathway issue.** Go to [File a New Pathway issue](https://github.com/WordPress/contributor-handbook/issues/new?template=2-new-pathway.yml), include the links and context you gathered, and submit. File an issue rather than editing the pathways index directly — the issue process ensures your idea gets proper review.

## Contribution checklist

- Idea is based on an existing process or team, or you've confirmed team support
- Searched the project board and confirmed it's not already there
- Gathered relevant links and context
- Filed a New Pathway issue with all the information

## What happens next

Your suggestion goes to the [project board](https://github.com/orgs/WordPress/projects/282) for review. You can volunteer to [write the guide](https://make.wordpress.org/handbook/pathways/write/create-a-pathway-guide/) yourself, or someone else from the community can pick it up.

## Help

Stuck? Check the [getting help guide](https://make.wordpress.org/handbook/pathways/before-you-begin/#getting-help), then ask in [#core-program](https://wordpress.slack.com/archives/core-program).
