# Organize: Suggest a Pathway

**Function:** Organize  
**Type:** Task

Help grow the pathways index by suggesting a new contribution pathway. You can point to an existing process already documented in a team handbook, or share a contribution idea that's supported by a team.

## Before you start

Complete the [common setup](/handbook/contribution-pathways/before-you-begin/) first, then:

- **Read:** Review [About Pathways](/pathways/about/) to understand what makes a good one, and review [TEMPLATE.md](https://github.com/WordPress/contributor-handbook/blob/main/pathways/TEMPLATE.md)
- **Connect:** Connect in [#core-program](https://wordpress.slack.com/archives/C09EEAUJFQS)

## Steps

1. First, search the [Pathways project board](https://github.com/orgs/WordPress/projects/282). If your idea is already there, feel free to add feedback or begin drafting a guide.
2. If your idea is not in the project already, [file a New Pathway issue](https://github.com/WordPress/contributor-handbook/issues/new/choose). Include links and context, then click Create.

## Contribution checklist
- [ ] The idea does not already exist in the index or [Pathways project board](https://github.com/orgs/WordPress/projects/282)
- [ ] The idea is supported by a handbook process or a team
- [ ] Filed a New Pathway issue with required fields and context

## What happens next
Your suggestion enters the [project board](https://github.com/orgs/WordPress/projects/282) for review. You're welcome to volunteer to write a guide yourself, or someone else from the community can pick it up.

## Help

Stuck? Check the [getting help guide](/handbook/contribution-pathways/before-you-begin/#getting-help), then ask in slack.
