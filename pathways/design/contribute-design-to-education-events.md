# Contribute Design to Education Events

Design badges, graphics, and event materials for WordPress education programs like Campus Connect and WP Credits. Your work directly supports programs that bring new contributors into the WordPress project.

- **Reference:** [WordPress Education Programs project board](https://github.com/orgs/WordPress/projects/277/views/1) — browse open design requests
- **Connect:** Join [#campusconnect](https://wordpress.slack.com/archives/campusconnect) and [#wpcredits](https://wordpress.slack.com/archives/wpcredits) on Slack and introduce yourself

## Steps

1. **Set up Figma.** Create a free [Figma](https://www.figma.com/) account if you don't have one.

2. **Find a design request.** Go to the [WordPress Education Programs project board](https://github.com/orgs/WordPress/projects/277/views/1) and filter by the Design label. If no requests are available, check back later or ask in #campusconnect or #wpcredits.

3. **Claim the request.** Choose a request from the Ready column and leave a comment to introduce yourself and let others know you're working on it. If the request is already In Progress, leave a comment and wait for acknowledgment before starting.

4. **Notify the Slack channels.** Share the issue link in #campusconnect or #wpcredits so organizers are aware — GitHub notifications can be missed.

5. **Start designing.** Clarify requirements (size, format, deadline) in the issue comments, and share early drafts for feedback before finalizing.

6. **Submit your design.** Comment on the issue with your final file, a Figma link if applicable, and exported assets in the requested format (usually SVG unless otherwise specified).

7. **Wait for approval.** The requester may approve the design, request revisions, or mark the issue as Done. Once approved and marked Done, your contribution is complete.

## Contribution checklist

- Design approved by the requester
- Issue marked Done
- Final file uploaded or linked in the issue

## What happens next

The requester reviews your submission directly in the GitHub issue. This can take a few days. If you haven't heard back after 48 hours, leave a follow-up comment on the issue tagging the requester. If you still don't hear back, post in #campusconnect or #wpcredits.

Once your contribution is complete, you can pick up another request from the board or explore other [Design pathways](https://make.wordpress.org/handbook/pathways/).

## Help

Stuck? Check the [getting help guide](https://make.wordpress.org/handbook/pathways/before-you-begin/#getting-help), then ask in [#campusconnect](https://wordpress.slack.com/archives/campusconnect) or [#wpcredits](https://wordpress.slack.com/archives/wpcredits) on Slack.
