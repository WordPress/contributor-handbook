# Join the Photos Moderation Team

You'll review community-submitted photos and approve or decline them based on quality and content guidelines. Moderators keep the WordPress Photo Directory a reliable resource for contributors worldwide.

- **Prerequisite:** [Contribute to Photos](https://make.wordpress.org/handbook/pathways/design/contribute-to-photos/) with at least 30 published photos
- **Reference:** [Moderating Photos](https://make.wordpress.org/photos/handbook/moderating-photos/)
- **Connect:** Join [#photos](https://wordpress.slack.com/archives/photos) on Slack and introduce yourself

## Steps

1. **Apply to join the moderation team.** Read the [Moderating Photos handbook page](https://make.wordpress.org/photos/handbook/moderating-photos/) and fill out the form to apply. Wait for a confirmation email.

2. **Open the moderation queue.** Log in to the [WordPress Photo Directory](https://wordpress.org/photos/) and navigate to the moderation queue.

3. **Review a photo.** Select a photo and verify it meets the [content and quality guidelines](https://wordpress.org/photos/guidelines/).

4. **Approve or decline.** If you decline, leave a clear reason so the contributor knows what to improve.

5. **Work in small batches.** When there are multiple photos to review, review one photo at a time rather than rushing through.

## Contribution checklist

- Applied and been approved to join the moderation team
- Completed your first moderation review

## What happens next

Your moderation decisions take effect immediately. Continue to moderate regularly and build familiarity with edge cases in the guidelines.

## Help

Stuck? Check the [getting help guide](https://make.wordpress.org/handbook/pathways/before-you-begin/#getting-help), then ask in [#photos](https://wordpress.slack.com/archives/photos). The Photos team leads keep an eye on the queue and can answer questions if you're unsure about a call. If you don't get a response in Slack within a few days, post again — the channel is active.

**Further reading:**
- [Photo Guidelines](https://wordpress.org/photos/guidelines/)
- [WordPress Photo Directory](https://wordpress.org/photos/)
