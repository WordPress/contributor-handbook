# Join the Patterns Moderation Team

**Function:** Design  
**Type:** Team  
**Level:** Intermediate

You'll review community-submitted block patterns and approve or decline them based on quality, uniqueness, and usefulness. Moderators keep the Block Pattern Directory a reliable resource for contributors worldwide.

## Before you start

Complete the [common setup](https://make.wordpress.org/handbook/pathways/before-you-begin/) first, then:

- **Setup:** Complete [Design — Submit Block Patterns](https://make.wordpress.org/handbook/pathways/design/submit-block-patterns/) with at least 10 published patterns
- **Read:** [Submit your block pattern to the directory](https://wordpress.org/documentation/article/submit-your-block-pattern-to-the-directory/) to review requirements
- **Connect:** Post in [#patterns](https://wordpress.slack.com/archives/patterns) that you'd like to help review patterns

## Steps

1. Once approved, log in to [WordPress.org](https://wordpress.org) and access the pattern moderation queue.
2. Select a pattern to review and verify it meets the guidelines: unique, useful, works across themes, and uses appropriate content.
3. Test the pattern in [WordPress Playground](https://wordpress.org/playground/) with different themes to verify it works as expected.
4. Approve or decline based on your assessment. If you decline, select a reason so the contributor knows what to improve.
5. When there are multiple patterns to review, work in small batches and review one pattern at a time.

## Contribution checklist

- Successfully joined the moderation team
- Completed your first batch of pattern reviews
- Continue to review patterns regularly

## What happens next

Your moderation decisions take effect immediately. Continue to moderate regularly and build familiarity with edge cases.
